About Us

The IHS Dev Team is a club at Ithaca High School dedicated to learning programming and expanding our knowledge through competitions and project development. In-club, we collaborate on projects, discuss topics and make decisions on upcoming or current events and activities, but the majority of our efforts are directed towards preparing for programming competitions such as PClassic and the St. Bonaventure and ACSL contests.
This website was created to practice Javascript, CSS and website programming.
Interested in joining? No experience is required and all are welcome! We meet in Room H-211 on Thursdays, and information, coding help and snacks are available.
